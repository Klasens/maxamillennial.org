So Soon I'll Retire

I'm nearly there, so soon I'll be hired. And then onto what's next, just waiting to retire. My network is full, my prospects so bright And yet I still sit here just longing to write. Or maybe it's to soar, to play, to live, then die. What if I'm being told, or sold, one simple lie? So melodramatic, yes, I certainly need to stop. Plus I have work in the morning, shift one till shift drop. It's been nearly ten, twenty, thirty years now And I feel I've lost all I'd hoped I'd found. But still something waits there, so deep, this fire. I simply can't wait, for so soon I'll retire.
