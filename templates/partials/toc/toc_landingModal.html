Welcome

Below are some details about this project that will help you navigate this website.

Maxamillennial.org is a collection of writing and photography. It's a story that uses the individual to ask questions about the self. Questions like, 'Who are we?' or 'Who are you?'

Ultimately, the intention of this work isn't to provide answers. Instead, it's to show that these questions reflect on each of us differently, and it's to explore the questions themselves with a hope, almost a need, to find something deeper.
