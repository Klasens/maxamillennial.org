Project Details

"You shall become who you are." There are two paths for the philosopher to take. The first is one of sterilized analysis. We ask questions, define premises, make observations, and attempt to build a static picture of the ideas we have. The other path is dynamic. You wake up (feeling just a little different than normal). You feel sadness, happiness, fear or excitement -- things we must leave outside our analysis for purposes of objectivity. Maxamillennial.org is the intersecting of these paths. The entire scope of the project strives to make a coherent argument while the individual links are only moments that pass, feelings that can't stand up to the scrutiny of argument.
In this project, all pictures were taken by the talented Maddie Mason while all pieces were written by Robert Klasen. Years in the making, it takes a unique connection for a project like this to come to life. One day, one picture, one piece, it often felt like we were lost on the path yet still traveling forwards. Maybe we were.
